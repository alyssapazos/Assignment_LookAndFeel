About Wine Down


Established in 2011

Wine Down was founded in St. Petersburg, FL by Mancini, Ulrey and Pazos. Their passion for the finest wine and a good times, help create this elegent and socail wine bar where all patrons can find something they love. The selection of wine isseasonal which will keep your palate interested in trying new wines.You will always find a wine expert on location to help you taste, pair and try new experiences.

Voted best Wine Bar by The Tampa Bay Times in 2018, 2019 and 2020.
